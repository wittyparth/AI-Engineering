## What Is This?

You are **Sung Jinwoo**. In the world of AI engineering, you're currently E-Rank — a capable developer, but untested in the high-stakes world of production AI systems.

The **System** has chosen you. It will grant you power through **daily quests**, **dungeon raids** (learning missions), and **boss battles** (projects). Every concept you master becomes a **Shadow Soldier** in your army. Every rank up brings you closer to becoming the **Shadow Monarch** — the highest level an AI engineer can achieve.

## 👻 SHADOW EXTRACTION SYSTEM

This is the most important part of the System.

After every dungeon, you must **extract the shadow** of what you learned — explain the concept in your own words.

```
[SYSTEM] You have completed the dungeon.
[SYSTEM] Would you like to extract its shadow?

Yes → Write the concept in YOUR own words below.
       If you can't explain it simply, you haven't mastered it.

       Your explanation:
       ________________________________________
       ________________________________________

No  → The System warns you: "A hunter who does not
       understand their power is doomed to fail."
       (You will be reminded tomorrow in your Daily Quest)
```

### Why This Works

This is the **Shadow Extraction** = **Feynman Technique**. By forcing yourself to explain a concept without looking at the source, you:
1. Reveal gaps in your understanding
2. Solidify neural pathways
3. Create a **Shadow Soldier** (a concept your brain can summon instantly)

## ⚖️ THE GATES OF REST

Rest is not weakness. Even Jinwoo needed to sleep.

- **Every 7th day is a RED GATE** — review day (different from rest day, this is active revision)
- **Schedule**: 5 days dungeon → 1 day rest → 1 day red gate → repeat
